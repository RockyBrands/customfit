Filtering Options

Below you will find a general guideline to the various ways you can filter the product view on your CustomFit site.

Top-level / Gender Filter

The broadest filtering option on the site.

Pricing Filter

Allows filtering based on bucketed price points or customer input.

Brand Filter

Allows filtering based on available brands in your company catalog.

Toe Type Filter

Allows filtering based on available toe types in your catalog.

Metatarsal Guard Filter

Allows filtering based on available metatarsal guards in your catalog.

Safety Feature Filter

Allows filtering based on available safety features in your company catalog.

Shoe Style Filter

Allows filtering based on available shoe styles in your company catalog.

Color / Size / Width

Allows filtering based on available colors, sizes and widths in your company catalog.
