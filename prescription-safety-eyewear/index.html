The Partnership

Lehigh CustomFit has established itself s THE leader in MANAGED PPE by being a digital data-driven program solution while consistently introducing new technology and services to the industrial workforce - and now we have a new vision.

Who is Bollé Saefty

For over 135 years, Bollé Safety has been at the forefront of PPE eyewear, evolving into a global leader in the industry.

With a relentless passion to design eyewear that people love to wear. Bollé has never lost sight of the ultimate mission: Protecting peoples' vision.

The Managed Prescription Safety Eyewear Program

The unique service of a Lehigh CustomFit managed program combined with the expert knowledge and advanced virtual fit technology of Bollé Safety is a perfect fit for any company providing Safety Eyewear. Together, we make optimal vision protection simple, accessible, and revolutionary.

The seemless solution ensures employees get the eyewear they need without the hassle, all backed by trusted experts & certified products.
