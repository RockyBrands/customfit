EIPP

Electronic Invoice Presentment & Payment

We make your life easier and help conserve our natural resources with electronic invoices, receipts, and payment. As a Lehigh customer, you'll automatically have it all continuously at your fingertips through Electronic Invoice Presentment & Payment (EIPP).

  • No more lost invoices
  • Review invoices online
  • No more waiting for the mail
  • Account access 24/7/365
  • Invoice notices emailed daily
  • Can be sent to more than one person in your company
  • Pay online by credit card or echeck
  • Receive payment verification
  • Less keying by your employees

How To Access Your Account Statements

After your first Lehigh CustomFit purchase, you will you will automatically receive an email notification with an invoice PDF file. You can then review this invoice and other account information online by logging into your EIPP account.

Any questions specifically and only regarding your EIPP account, can be sent to here, or contact us by chat or phone. (Please do not send payment information to this address)
