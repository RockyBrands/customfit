Payment Options

For your convenience and ease, we have several payment options from which to choose. We have the experience and know-how to implement your Subsidy Program to avoid hassle and confusion during invoicing and payment. We can also incorporate a Payroll Deduction Plan with any remaining balance. We'll customize to your company's distinctive payment methods or specific subsidy allotment. All options include:

  • Credit Card Only
  • Payroll Deduction
  • Specific Percentage Up To A Dollar Amount
  • Dollar Subsidy
  • Percentage Subsidy
  • 100% Invoice
